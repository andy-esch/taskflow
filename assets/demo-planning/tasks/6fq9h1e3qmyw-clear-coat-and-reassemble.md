# Clear-coat and reassemble

## Objective

Seal the neon under a durable clear coat, let it fully cure, then rebuild the bike
with fresh bearings. Waiting on the base coats to go down and cure first.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Clear coat sprayed and left to cure hard
- [ ] Frame wet-sanded and buffed
- [ ] Bike reassembled with fresh bearings

## Out of scope

- A polish and wax routine — that's ongoing care, not the build.
