# Prime and sand frame

## Objective

Lay down an even etch primer and wet-sand it smooth so the neon base goes on
glass-flat with no bare spots at the welds.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] Etch primer coats sprayed
- [ ] Primer wet-sanded to 600 grit
- [ ] Welds and dropouts checked for coverage

## Out of scope

- Filler primer for deep scratches — none deep enough to need it.
