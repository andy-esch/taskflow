# Spray neon base coats

## Objective

Lay the neon color in thin, even passes over a white base so it reads bright and
uniform, letting each coat flash off before the next.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] White base coat down for punch
- [ ] Neon sprayed in thin even passes
- [ ] Coverage even under shop light and daylight

## Out of scope

- Masked graphics or fades — a solid neon this round.
