# BMX neon paint job

## Goal

Strip a scuffed BMX frame to bare metal and repaint it in a neon finish under a
durable clear coat — a look that pops at the skatepark and survives it.

## Why this is its own epic

Paint is a strict pipeline — strip, prime, base, clear — where each stage has to
cure before the next, so the steps are tracked in order.

## Out of scope

- New components — the build stays; only the frame gets refinished.
