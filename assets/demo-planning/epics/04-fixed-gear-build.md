# Fixed-gear build

## Goal

Build a fixed-gear commuter up from a bare frame — a clean, low-maintenance
single-speed for daily city miles.

## Why this is its own epic

A fresh build from parts, so the remaining work is small and self-contained: get
the contact points and the chainline dialed.

## Out of scope

- A geared freewheel conversion — this bike is fixed by design.
